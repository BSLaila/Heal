Welcome to Heal

Heal was created in April 2023 with the sole goal of making meditation and inner wellbeing more accessible.
Heal's founder, Laila, discovered meditation and its benefits after a challenging time in her life.
Meditation allowed her to sustain her stressful work on a daily basis and keep getting her paycheck monthly, which provided her with a roof and some food.
Seeing how much meditation helped her, she started sharing her knowlege and ultimately organize Wellness Retreats to help others.
Since then Heal has become known for their community and their support.
